Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Avyro.ai ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered fitness coaching application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using Avyro.ai, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information: Name, email address
  • Health and biometric data: Heart rate variability (HRV), sleep duration and quality, resting heart rate, daily step count, and recovery metrics — sourced from Apple HealthKit, Google Health Connect, Garmin, and Oura
  • Workout data: Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, session duration, and workout history
  • Location data: Approximate location used for displaying nearby training groups on the map
  • Chat messages: Conversations with the AI coach and messages sent in group chats
  • Photos: Images you choose to share in group chats

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Personalised training: Your health and biometric data is used to generate and adapt your AI-coached training program in real time
  • AI coaching: Chat messages and biometric context are sent to our AI provider to generate personalised coaching responses
  • Group features: Your name, streak, and shared messages/photos are visible to members of groups you join
  • Location features: Your approximate location is used to display nearby training groups on the map and to contextualise AI coaching advice
  • Progress tracking: Workout logs and streak data are stored to show your training history and achievements

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS encryption.

AI coaching messages are processed by Anthropic (Claude API). Messages are sent to Anthropic for generating responses but are not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our app:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Powers the AI coaching conversations
  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and real-time features
  • Google Maps: Map display for group location features
  • Apple HealthKit: Health data integration on iOS devices
  • Google Health Connect: Health data integration on Android devices

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Data shared with third-party services is limited to what is necessary to provide the described functionality.

6. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data — including your profile, workout history, chat messages, and health data — will be permanently deleted within 48 hours of the deletion request.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data export: Request an export of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@avyro.app.

8. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly within the app via Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon confirmation, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing privacy@avyro.app.

9. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following lawful bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when you create an account and connect health data sources
  • Legitimate interest: We process data as necessary to improve and maintain the service, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Contract performance: Processing is necessary to deliver the AI coaching service you have signed up for

You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us at privacy@avyro.app.

10. Children's Privacy

Avyro.ai is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@avyro.app and we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@avyro.app
  • Website: https://avyro.app
