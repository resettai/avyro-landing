AVYRO.AI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Avyro.ai ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered fitness coaching application operated from Norway. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Norwegian data protection law.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, the purposes for which we process it, who we share it with, and the rights you have in relation to your data. By creating an account and using Avyro.ai, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data when you use Avyro.ai:

Account information

  • Your name and email address, provided when you create an account

Fitness and health data

  • Heart rate variability (HRV), sleep duration and quality, and resting heart rate sourced from connected wearables and health platforms
  • Workout history including exercises, sets, reps, weights, and session duration

AI coaching conversations

  • Messages you send to and receive from the AI coach, stored to maintain conversation context and improve your coaching experience

Location data

  • City-level approximate location used for weather context, timezone adjustments, and AI coaching personalisation. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.

Device and usage data

  • Device type, operating system version, and crash logs collected via Sentry for stability and bug-fixing purposes
  • In-app usage patterns collected through our own custom analytics system (we do not use third-party tracking services such as Google Analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Personalised coaching: Your health metrics, workout history, and chat messages are used to generate and adapt your AI training programme in real time
  • AI response generation: Your chat messages and relevant biometric context are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to produce coaching responses tailored to your current physical state
  • Progress tracking: Workout logs, streaks, and health trends are stored so you can review your training history and achievements
  • Notifications: With your consent, we send push notifications via Expo Notifications to remind you of workouts, share readiness updates, or deliver coaching messages
  • App stability: Crash reports and device information are used to identify and fix bugs, ensuring a reliable experience
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymised usage data helps us understand how features are used and where improvements are needed

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database running on secure cloud infrastructure. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include access controls, encrypted backups, and regular security reviews.

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security incident, please contact us immediately at privacy@avyro.app.

5. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate Avyro.ai:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Powers the AI coaching conversations. Your chat messages and relevant biometric context are sent to Anthropic to generate responses. Anthropic does not use your data to train their models.
  • Supabase: Provides our database, user authentication, and real-time data synchronisation
  • Sentry: Collects crash reports and device information (device model, OS version, error stack traces) to help us diagnose and fix bugs
  • Expo Notifications: Delivers push notifications to your device when you have opted in
  • Apple HealthKit (iOS): Provides read-only access to health data you have chosen to share
  • Google Health Connect (Android): Provides read-only access to health data you have chosen to share

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. Data shared with third-party services is strictly limited to what is necessary to provide the functionality described above. We do not use any third-party advertising or behavioural tracking services.

6. Health Data

We treat your health data with the highest level of care. Health data accessed through Apple HealthKit and Google Health Connect is subject to the following commitments:

  • Health data is accessed on a read-only basis. We do not write data back to HealthKit or Health Connect.
  • Health data is never sold, shared with, or disclosed to third parties for advertising, marketing, or data-brokering purposes
  • Health data is used solely to personalise your AI coaching experience, including readiness scoring, workout adaptation, and recovery recommendations
  • Biometric context (such as current HRV and sleep hours) may be included in prompts sent to the Anthropic API to generate relevant coaching advice. No raw health data exports are shared externally.
  • You can disconnect health data sources at any time from your device settings, and previously synced health data will be deleted from our servers upon account deletion

We comply fully with Apple's HealthKit and Google's Health Connect usage policies. Your health data is never used for purposes unrelated to providing you with personalised fitness coaching within Avyro.ai.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Avyro.ai is operated from Norway, within the European Economic Area (EEA). Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data (see Section 9 below)
  • Right to data portability: You can request an export of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Right to restrict processing: You can request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the Norwegian Data Protection Authority (Datatilsynet) or another supervisory authority in your country of residence

Legal bases for processing

We process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): You provide explicit consent when creating your account, connecting health data sources, and enabling push notifications
  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary to deliver the AI coaching service you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): We process data as necessary to maintain service stability, prevent abuse, and improve the app, where these interests are not overridden by your rights

To exercise any of your rights, please contact us at privacy@avyro.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by GDPR.

8. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers (including Anthropic and Sentry) may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission for the recipient country
  • Other legally recognised transfer mechanisms under GDPR

You may request further details about the safeguards in place by contacting us at privacy@avyro.app.

9. Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for as long as your account is active and necessary to provide you with the Avyro.ai service. Specifically:

  • Account and profile data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Workout history and health data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Chat messages: Retained until you delete your account. Messages sent to the Anthropic API are not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request.
  • Crash reports: Retained by Sentry for up to 90 days
  • Analytics data: Stored in aggregated, anonymised form and retained indefinitely for service improvement

When you delete your account, all associated personal data — including your profile, workout history, chat messages, and synced health data — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

10. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app by navigating to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon confirmation, your data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems within 30 days.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing privacy@avyro.app from the email address associated with your account.

11. Children's Privacy

Avyro.ai is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16 years of age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that data as promptly as possible.

If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at privacy@avyro.app.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through an in-app notification before the changes take effect.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was most recently revised. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes your acknowledgement of the updated policy.

13. Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@avyro.app
  • Website: https://avyro.app

If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Norwegian Data Protection Authority (Datatilsynet) at www.datatilsynet.no.
